How signs work

On the signs test you identify each sign by its shape and color and say what it means. Learn the shape and color system first, because it lets you decode signs you have never seen.

Red - stop or prohibited Yellow - general warning Orange - work zone Green - guide or permitted Blue - motorist services Brown - recreation or scenic

Sign shapes at a glance

Regulatory signs

These tell you the law: what you must or must not do. Mostly white rectangles, plus the red stop, yield, and do-not-enter shapes.

Warning signs

Yellow diamonds and special shapes warning of a hazard or change ahead. Orange means a work zone.

Guide & service signs

Green guides you to places, blue marks driver services, brown marks parks and recreation.
